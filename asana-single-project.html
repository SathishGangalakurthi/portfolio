About the Project:

This was my first project, developed during the initial module of my course. I aimed to create a yoga studio website featuring sections for Home, About, Shop, Blog, and Contact. Each section smoothly navigates to its corresponding content. The Shop section includes yoga-related products such as mats, straps, and meditation accessories.

The design of the website is minimalist and user-friendly, utilizing Bootstrap to ensure responsiveness and visual appeal. Overall, it promotes mindfulness and encourages continuous personal growth through yoga practices, creating an inviting and supportive environment for users to explore.
